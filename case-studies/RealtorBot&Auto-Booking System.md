## **How It Works ‚Äî The AI Edge**

1. **User Query** ‚Üí User enters a query or preference about properties  
2. **AI Qualification** ‚Üí The AI asks follow-up questions (if needed) and qualifies the lead based on intent:  
   - **Hot leads**: Ready to buy, rent, or invest now  
   - **Warm leads**: Interested, but not urgent  
   - **Cold leads**: Browsing or uncertain  
3. **Property Search & Matching** ‚Üí AI queries a structured database for properties based on user filters (location, budget, bedrooms, etc.)
4. **Agent Notification** ‚Üí If the lead is hot, an automatic notification is sent to the agent with the lead‚Äôs summary and details of matched properties.
5. **Instant Handoff** ‚Üí High-value leads get immediate attention from agents via **WhatsApp or email** ‚Äî no delays, no wasted time.

‚úÖ Every **qualified lead** is **delivered instantly** to agents, ready for follow-up.
